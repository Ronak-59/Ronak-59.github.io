Food Recognition System

Foodify aims to solve the major food identification problem of the tourists visiting foreign countries as well as to make them aware of the food nutrients and where it would be available in the nearby restaurants. It also helps the user to order food though the website. Foodify describes minimum eight and maximum twenty food ingredients and its nutritional information from the uploaded photo.

Foodify describes the usefulness, systematic flow of work and the working of the Web App. It aims to give a detailed description of Food, Image Recognition, Nutritional Information and other accompanying features. Foodify help users recognize various food items with image scanning and image processing. It also will provide the user with the nutritional information about the scanned food items and help them find restaurants having these food items.
